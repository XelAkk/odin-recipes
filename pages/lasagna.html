Best lasagna in the universe

Ingredients:

Bechamel:

  • 1l milk
  • 80g butter (unsalted)
  • 80g wheat flour
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Lasag:

  • 250g mozarella
  • 400g minced meat (beef or 50/50 with pork)
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 onions
  • 2 celery stalks
  • 70g tomato paste
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 3-4 tbsp oil
  • lasagna sheets
  • salt and peper depending on taste

Dishes:

  • 1 baking dish
  • 1 big pot for bolognese

How to cook:

  1. Make bechamel.
  2. Finely dice all the vegetables.
  3. Sear minced meat in a pot while breaking it up to avoid meat clumps.
  4. Add all of the vegetables and spices to the pot along with a can of tomatoes and 2-3 cups of water.
  5. Let it simmer for several hours or until the contents of the sauce start resembling sauce instead of soup.
  6. Assemble lasagna in the baking dish starting with a layer of sauce.
  7. Alternate between bechamel, bolognese and pasta sheets until you run out of ingridients or space in your bakeware.
  8. Finish with a layer of shredded cheese.
  9. Bake lasagna in the oven at 180C for 35-40 minutes.
  10. You can finish cooking lasagna under a broiler if cheese hasn't browned yet.

Coongratulations!
