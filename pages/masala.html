Best tea in the universe

recipe source

Ingredients:

  • 50g dried ginger
  • 5g nutmeg
  • 15g cinnamon
  • 10g black peppercorns
  • 15g clove
  • 15g green cardamom
  • 4-5 black cardamom pods
  • black tea
  • milk

Dishes:

  • 1 frying pan
  • 1 saucepan
  • 1 blender for spices / kitchen processor

How to cook:

  1. Dry roast all spices except ginger in a pan on low-medium flame for about a minute.
  2. Grind all the spices into a powder.
  3. Keep your masala powder in an airtight container.
  4. To make masala tea add 1 cup of water to a saucepan along witg 1 tsp of masala powder and 1-2 tsp of your favourite black tea to taste. Boil the tea for 1 minute.
  5. Add milk and let the tea boil for another 3-8 minutes (depending on how strong you like your tea).
  6. Strain the tea and add sugar/honey.

Done!
